Fundraising

With over 20 members eager to attend nationals, Ithaca High School TSA needs to raise thousands of dollars to pay for a bus to take ourselves, luggage, and great hopes for success to Nashville, Tennessee, for the Technology Student Association national competition. Much of the roughly $650 cost per student will be paid for by parents—but to offset this as much as possible, we see Gertrude Hawk chocolate bars to our fellow students for $1 per bar. Our most successful sellers have made hundreds of dollars on this.
