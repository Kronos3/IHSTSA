Who we are

That’s IHSTSA’s purpose, clear and simple. We don’t muck around with some traditional TSA activities like holding meetings with parliamentary procedure or community service, because we believe the best use of our time is learning more about STEM. With many IHSTSA alumni going on to study in STEM fields at top-notch colleges, like UC Berkeley and traditionally, Cornell University, IHSTSA alumni have some of the best chances at making the world better. Hopefully, if IHSTSA alumni go on to make the world a better place though STEM, IHSTSA can claim to have contributed as much as possible to their education.

Also, we’re about fun. Lots of fun. And of course there are the quirks that make us who we are, like the tradition that no two officer team presentations may use the same theme.
