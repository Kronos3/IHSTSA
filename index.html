Who we are

Aspiring engineers. Aspiring scientists. Students aspiring to technical excellence in pursuit of a better world. IHSTSA members push the envelope when it comes to STEM education in order to better prepare themselves for the future.

What we do

Fundamentally, IHSTSA is one of the many chapters that make up the Technology Student Association in the New York. For years, IHSTSA has been a standout organization that helps young high school students realize their dreams and design goals in STEM. Around 30 students identify as a member of IHSTSA. However, many of these students take no engineering course in school. Instead, these students have found their love in the countless events in TSA and the teamwork involved in the chapter.

That’s IHSTSA’s purpose, clear and simple. We don’t spend time on holding meetings with parliamentary procedure; we believe the best use of our time is learning more about STEM. In addition, we focus on benefitting the community by doing community service--helping out at marathons, STEM demonstrations, and going to schools to help young kids learn about technology. With many IHSTSA alumni going on to study in STEM fields at top-notch colleges, such as UC Berkeley and Cornell University, all IHSTSA members go on to changing the world for the better. Our chapter plays a vital role in preparing students for real-world scenarios that can lead to world changing discoveries and contributions to STEM.

We pride ourselves in being great. For two years in a row, IHSTSA has won High School Chapter of the Year at the State Conference. Moreover, almost every single one of our students placed in at least one event at the State Conference. At the National Conference, our success does not stop. Annually, our chapter places in various events and semifinals in two times more.

However, our main goal as a chapter is to have fun. TSA is supposed to be a place to meet new friends, create new connections, and enjoy creating the best projects possible. As students are aspiring to be scientists, doctors, engineers, and teachers, IHSTSA offers a fun medium when it comes to working with others and developing skills in technology.
