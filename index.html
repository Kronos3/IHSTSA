Ithaca High School Technology Student Association

Every year we compete in Technology Student Association competitions.


Our Modified Creed: The Technology Education holds an important place in our lives in the technical world. There is a need for the development of good attitudes concerning work, tools, materials, experimentation, and processes of industry. We will accept the responsibilities that are ours and the theories that are supported by proper evidence. We will explore on our own for safer, more effective methods of working and living. We will strive to be fun and respectful. Through our work, we will express our ideas to the best of our ability.

In short, we will strive to make the world a better place in which to live.
